## General Information

| Attribute               | Value |
|-------------------------|-------|
| **Owner**               | signal_graph |
| **Applied Stereotype**  |   |

## Enumeration Literals
| Name          | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------------|
| RedundancyFor | <p> <i>RedundancyFor</i> specifies, that <i>&quot;from&quot;-SignalGraph </i>of a <i>SignalGraphRelation</i> is a redundant implementation for the <i>&quot;to&quot;-SignalGraph.</i>      </p> |
