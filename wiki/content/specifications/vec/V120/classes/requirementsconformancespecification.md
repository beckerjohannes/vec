<html>   <head>     </head>   <body> A <i>RequirementsConformanceSpecification </i>can be used to express the conformance of a <i>PartVersion </i>(or a group of <i>PartVersions</i>). The <i>PartVersions</i> to which this <i>RequirementsConformanceSpecification</i> applies are expressed through the <i>describedPart </i>reference.</body> </html>
